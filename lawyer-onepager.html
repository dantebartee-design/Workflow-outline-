What you'll receive
  • The deliverable. Clean, finished, and ready to use — no assembly required.
  • A simple playbook. A short, plain-English note on what was done and how to use it.
  • Status updates. Brief check-ins at agreed points, so you're never left wondering where things stand.
What helps me help you
  • Send documents & deadlines up front. The sooner I have them, the faster I can move.
  • Flag anything unusual early. Case-specific quirks are best known before, not after.
  • Tell me the outcome you want. The goal, not just the task — it helps me get it right the first time.
  • Don't polish it for me. Formatting and cleanup are my job — just send what you have.
